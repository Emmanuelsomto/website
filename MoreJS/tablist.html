Planets

Earth is the only planet, known for its abundant water, diverse ecosystems, and life-supporting atmosphere. It's the only planet in the solar system known to habour life.

Saturn is famous for it's beautiful and extensive ring system made of ice and rock particles. It's a gas giant with dozens of moons orbiting it.

Mars, the red planet, is a rocky world with the tallest volcano and deepest canyon in the solar system. It's a key focus for exploration in the search for past or present life.
